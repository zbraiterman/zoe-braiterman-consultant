What Makes a Great Corporate Event?

I currently host the following events to empower founders in the product security space to achieve the following.

  • Establish credibility by positioning themselves and their co-founders or technical leaders as authoritative voices in product security
  • Build brand visibility through event promotion, agenda placement, social amplification, and post‑event content reuse
  • Position their startup as a long‑term player by showing up where the industry gathers to shape the future
